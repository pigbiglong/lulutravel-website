About Us

Discover the Real China

Small group tours (2-8 people) · Expert local guides · Hidden gems you won't find online

Our Story

We started lulutravel with a simple belief: China deserves to be seen differently. Not from a tour bus with 40 strangers, but walking ancient pathways with someone who knows every hidden corner.

Our guides grew up in China. They've walked the Great Wall before sunrise, know the best dumpling shop in every neighborhood, and have stories that guidebooks will never tell you.

We keep groups small (2-8 people) because big groups rush. Small groups wander. They stop when they see something interesting. They eat when they're hungry. They leave space for the unexpected — and that's where the best memories happen.
