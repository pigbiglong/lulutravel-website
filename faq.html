About Itineraries

Our small group tours are designed for 2-8 travelers, creating an intimate and personalized experience. Unlike large bus tours with 40+ people, our small groups allow for:

  • Flexible pacing and spontaneous discoveries
  • Access to exclusive venues and hidden gems
  • Meaningful connections with fellow travelers
  • Personalized attention from expert guides
  • Authentic local dining experiences

We specialize in three core themes, each crafted for immersive cultural exploration:

Classic China: Beijing, Xi'an, Shanghai — the essentials done right.

Culinary Journey: Sichuan cuisine, panda bases, and ancient food traditions.

Nature & Wellness: Zhangjiajie, Huangshan, and peaceful retreats.

About Booking

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for the best selection of dates and accommodations. Popular seasons (April-May, September-October) fill up quickly.

Last-minute bookings may be possible depending on availability.

  • 60+ days before departure: Full refund minus $200 fee
  • 30-59 days before departure: 50% refund
  • 15-29 days before departure: 25% refund
  • Within 14 days: No refund

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance.

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfer, and PayPal.

A deposit of $500 per person is required to secure your booking.

About Visas

Most travelers require a visa to enter mainland China. We provide invitation letters and all necessary documentation to support your visa application.

Citizens of select countries may enjoy visa-free entry. Please check current regulations.

Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your planned departure date and have at least two blank visa pages.
